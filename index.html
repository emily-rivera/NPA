What We Do

and you can do too!

Qualifying

Many women in Mozambique do not have the necessary education to gain employment. We have created vocational courses for these women to attend to produce an income for themselves and their families.

Educating

One of the main things that keeps girls out of school in Mozambique is not being able to afford a uniform. We now have a sponsorship program for these girls, which pays for school fees, school supplies, two uniforms and much more.

Rebuilding

On March 14 Mozambique was hit by Cyclone Idai. The cyclone took over 1,00 lives and more than 140,00 people were displaced from their homes. We are rebuilding homes and we need your help!

Nourishing

COVID-19 has taken a toll on the economy in Mozambique. Thousands of families in the areas we support are currently going hungry and have no where to turn. We want to help these families until their futures are once again secured.
